How It Works

Every bounty follows one of two transparent routes: instant payout when expectations are met, or neutral arbitration if the result is disputed.

Happy Path

1. Poster creates bounty and locks funds in escrow
2. Worker accepts scope and submits deliverable
3. Poster approves work before deadline
4. Contract releases payment to worker

Dispute Path

1. Poster creates bounty and escrow is funded
2. Worker submits output with proof
3. Either party raises dispute before auto-release
4. Arbitrator reviews evidence and rules
5. Contract sends payout according to ruling
